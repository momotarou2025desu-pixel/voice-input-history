Voice Input History - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-08

1. Overview

Voice Input History is an Android keyboard app (IME). This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles data.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information that identifies you (such as name, address, or phone number).

3. On-Device Storage (Memo / History / Clipboard History)

To provide the app’s features, the app may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Saved phrases (snippets / memo slots)
  • Input history (text you commit through the keyboard)
  • Clipboard history while the keyboard is active

This data is stored on your device (encrypted storage). We do not upload it to our servers. You can remove this data by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app.

4. Password Fields

Password fields are excluded from clipboard history collection.

5. Voice Input

If you enable voice input, your device’s speech recognition functionality (or the speech recognition service you choose) processes your audio to produce text. Voice Input History does not upload or store your audio on our servers.

6. Advertising and Analytics

Voice Input History does not use advertising SDKs or analytics tools.

7. Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

8. Permissions

  • RECORD_AUDIO: Used only if you enable voice input.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
